recipe: spinach artichoke spaghetti squash boats 
author: instagram.com/nutritionbymia

Ingredients:
1 spaghetti squash 
1 spinach and artichoke dip, thawed and sliced in half 
1 package of pre-cooked chicken, chopped 
Optional for serving: Parmesan and red pepper flakes 

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 400F.
2. Prick the spaghetti squash all over with a fork and microwave for 5-6 minutes to soften.
3. Slice in half lengthwise and remove the seeds.
4. Place the squash halves cut side up on a baking sheet.
5. Place one half of the spinach and artichoke dip in each squash half.
6. Bake for 30-40 minutes, until the squash is tender.
7. Use a fork to pull the strands away from the skin, then mix together with the dip and chopped chicken. Top with parmesan and red pepper flakes if using.
