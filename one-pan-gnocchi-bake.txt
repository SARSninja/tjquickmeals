recipe: one-pan gnocchi bake
author: instagram.com/nutritionbymia

Ingredients: 
1 lb ground beef
1 jar of rosatella pasta sauce
1 package of gnocchi 
1/3 cup shaved parmesan
1/4-1/3 cup chopped fresh basil 

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
2. Cook the beef in a skillet.
3. Stir in the sauce and gnocchi and cook for 3–4 minutes to start softening the gnocchi.
4. Top with parmesan, transfer to the oven and continue cooking for 10–12 minutes until bubbly and golden.
5. Serve with fresh basil.
