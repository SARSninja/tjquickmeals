recipe: chicken pot pie skillet 
author: instagram.com/nutritionbymia

Ingredients:
1 roll of biscuits
1 package of mirepoix 
1 broccoli and cheddar soup
1 package of pre-cooked chicken, chopped 
Salt and pepper 

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350F.
2. In a large oven-safe skillet, saute the mirepoix in a little oil and season with salt + pepper.
3. Stir in the chicken and soup until warmed through.
4. Top with biscuits and bake for 16-18 minutes, or until golden and bubbly.
