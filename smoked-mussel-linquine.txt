recipe: smoked mussel linguine
author: instagram.com/nutritionbymia

Ingredients:
1 can of smoked mussels 
3 garlic cloves, minced
8 oz. Linguine
1/4-1/3 cup cacio e pepe pasta sauce 
1/2-1 cup of flat leaf parsley, chopped
Optional for serving: fresh parmesan, red pepper flakes, 

Instructions:
1. Cook the pasta according to package directions until al dente. Reserve about 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain.
2. Saute the garlic in a skillet with a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat for 1 minute, until fragrant.
3. Add the smoked mussels (with their oil) and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
4. Stir in the Cacio e Pepe sauce, a splash (about 2 tablespoons) of pasta water, and the cooked linguine. Toss to combine and cook for another 2 minutes, until evenly coated and creamy.
5. Finish with parsley, toss again, and top with Parmesan and red pepper flakes if desired. Serve warm.
