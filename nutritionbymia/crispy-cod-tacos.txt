recipe: crispy cod tacos
author: instagram.com/nutritionbymia

Ingredients:
1 box Oven-Ready Breaded Cod Fillets
6 small flour tortillas
1 ripe avocado, sliced 
1 coleslaw Kit
2-4 tbsp Cilantro Dressing
Optional for serving: fresh cilantro 

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 425F. Place cod fillets on a parchment-lined baking sheet and bake for 18-20 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden and crisp.
2. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave until soft.
3. Toss together the colelsaw kit.
4. Assemble the tacos with the slaw, cod, avocado slices and a drizzle of cilantro dressing.
