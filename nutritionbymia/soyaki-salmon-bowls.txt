recipe: soyaki salmon bowls
author: instagram.com/nutritionbymia

Ingredients:
8 oz. salmon filet, diced
1/4 cup soyaki sauce
2 cups coleslaw lettuce
1 medium avocado
2 packages of pre-cooked jasmine rice 
Optional for serving: sesame seeds, more soyaki sauce

Instructions:
1. Toss the salmon with the soyaki sauce and air fry for 8-10 minutes at 400 F.
2. Microwave the rice according to package instructions and serve with the salmon, avocado and coleslaw lettuce.
