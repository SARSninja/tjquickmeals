recipe: Cheesy Chimichurri Melt Tacos
author: instagram.com/nutritionbymia

Ingredients: 
1 lb shaved beef
1 bag fire roasted bell peppers & onions
4–5 slices pepper jack cheese
8 small flour tortillas
½ cup chimichurri sauce (plus extra if desired)

Instructions:
1. Cook the beef in a skillet with oil for 2–3 minutes per side until browned, then remove.
2. Add 1 bag of fire roasted peppers and onions to the same skillet and cook for 6–7 minutes or until heated through.
3. Return the beef to the skillet and stir to combine.
4. Place 4–5 slices of pepper jack on top, cover, and cook for 2 minutes or until fully melted.
5. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet for 20–30 seconds per side to soften.
6. Top each tortilla with the beef and pepper mixture and spoon the chimichurri sauce over before serving.
