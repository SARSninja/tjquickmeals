recipe: Veggie noodle bowl
author: instagram.com/nutritionbymia

Ingredients:
1 package of squiggly noodles
1 cup shelled edamame
2 cups stir-fry veggie blend
2-3 tbsp soyaaki sauce
2-3 cups miso ginger broth 
Optional for topping: green onion, sesame seeds 

Instructions:
1. Place the noodles, veggies, edamame, soyaki sauce and broth in a  pot, cover and cook over medium heat for 6-8 minutes or  until the noodles are tender and veggies are heated through. 
2. Top with green onion and sesame seeds if desired.
