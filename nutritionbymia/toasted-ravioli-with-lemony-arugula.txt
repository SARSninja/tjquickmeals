recipe: toasted ravioli with lemony arugula 
author: instagram.com/nutritionbymia

Ingredients
1 lemony arugula basil salad kit
1 package of roasted cauliflower and cheese ravioli 
Optional for topping: fresh lemon juice and parmesan

Instructions:
1. Cook the ravioli in boiling water for 3-4 minutes until they float.
2. Transfer to a skillet and toast on each side for 1-2 minutes until golden.
3. Toss the salad kit ingredients together.
4. Plate with ravioli, top with fresh parmesan, and serve alongside the salad.
