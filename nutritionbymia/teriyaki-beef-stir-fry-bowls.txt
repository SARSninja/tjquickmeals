recipe: Teriyaki Beef Stir Fry Bowls
author: instagram.com/nutritionbymia

Ingredients:
1 package teriyaki beef skirt steak
1 package of Asian style vegetable stir fry
1 package of pre-cooked organic jasmine rice
1/4-1/3 cup soyaki sauce

Instructions:
1.  Cook the beef in a large skillet with oil over medium-high heat for about 3 minutes per side, or until it reaches an internal temperature of 145Â°F. Let it rest before slicing.
2.  In the same skillet, saute the veggies in oil for 5-7 minutes, or until crisp-tender. Add the sliced beef back in and stir in Soyaki sauce to taste.
3.  Microwave the rice for 3 minutes according to package instructions.
4.  Serve the beef and veggie mixture over rice.
