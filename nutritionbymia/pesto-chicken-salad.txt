recipe: Pesto Chicken Salad
author: instagram.com/nutritionbymia

Ingredients:
1 harvest apple salad kit
1 package of pesto chicken (~4 chicken breasts)
1/2 cup bruschetta sauce 
1 fresh mozzarella cheese ball 
Balsamic glaze 

Instructions:
1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the pesto chicken breasts for 4-5 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165F.
2. Place a slice of mozzarella on each chicken breast, cover, and cook for 2 minutes or until the cheese melts.
3. In a large bowl, toss together the salad kit ingredients. Divide the salad among 4 bowls.
4. Top each with the chicken, a spoonful of bruschetta sauce, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze.
