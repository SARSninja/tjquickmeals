Ingredients:
1 lb ground grass-fed beef
1/2-1 packet of taco seasoning 
6 small flour tortillas
1 cup shredded cheddar
1/2 cup Guacasalsa

Instructions:
1.  Preheat oven to 400Â°F.
2.  Brown the beef in a skillet and cook with the taco seasoning.
3.  Brush both sides of the tortillas with oil. Scoop the beef onto one half of each tortilla, sprinkle with cheese, fold over, and top with a little more cheese.
4.  Bake 8-10 minutes or until crisp.
5.  Drizzle with Guacasalsa and serve.
