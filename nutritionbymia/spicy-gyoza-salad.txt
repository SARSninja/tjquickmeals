recipe: spicy gyoza salad
author: instagram.com/nutritionbymia

Ingredients:
1 peanut and crispy noodle salad kit
3/4-1 bag of Chicken gyoza potstickers
2-3 tsp crunchy chili onion (scale up or down for desired heat)

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 400F.
2. Toss the potstickers with the crunchy chili onion and bake for 15-18 minutes, tossing halfway. 
3. Toss together the salad kit ingredients and  the potstickers and serve.
