recipe: beef and broccolini noodles
author: instagram.com/nutritionbymia

Ingredients:
8 oz. shaved beef
2 packets of Thai wheat noodles
1 bunch of broccolini 
1/2 cup general tsao stir fry sauce 

Instructions:
1. Saute the beef in a skillet over medium-high heat for 4-5 minutes until browned.
2. Add the broccolini to the pan and cook for 3-4 minutes, until tender-crisp.
3. Pour in the sauce and toss to combine.
4. Add in the noodles, stir well, and heat through.
