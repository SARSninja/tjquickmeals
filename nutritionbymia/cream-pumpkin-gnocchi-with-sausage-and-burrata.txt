recipe: Creamy Pumpkin Gnocchi with Spicy Sausage & Burrata
author: instagram.com/nutritionbymia

Ingredients
1 package of pumpkin gnocchi
1 package of burrata 
1/2 lb. spicy sausage, casing removed
2 cups spinach, chopped
1 jar of Autumnal Harvest Creamy Pasta Sauce
Optional for serving: red pepper flakes 

Instructions:
1. Cook the gnocchi according to package directions.
2. In a large skillet, cook and crumble the sausage. Add in spinach, cooked gnocchi, and pasta sauce. Stir to combine and cook until heated through.
3. Serve with burrata.
