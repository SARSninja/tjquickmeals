recipe: bang bang shrimp bowls
author: instagram.com/nutritionbymia

Ingredients
8 oz. cooked shrimp (from the refrigerated section)
3 cups coleslaw lettuce
Â¼ cup mayonnaise
3 tablespoons sweet chili sauce
2 packets pre-cooked rice
Optional for topping: green onion, sesame seeds 

Instructions:
1. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise and sweet chili sauce to make the sauce.
2. Toss the coleslaw lettuce with half of the sauce. 
3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add shrimp and saute for 2-3 minutes until warmed through. Stir in half of the sauce to coat.
4. Microwave the rice according to package instructions and divide between two bowls.
5. Top each bowl with coleslaw and shrimp.
