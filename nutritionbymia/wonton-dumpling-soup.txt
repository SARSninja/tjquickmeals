recipe: wonton dumpling soup
author: instagram.com/nutritionbymia

Ingredients: 
1 bunch of bok choy, chopped
1 package of  shiitake mushrooms, sliced
1 package chicken cilantro mini wontons (about 12–16 wontons)
1 cup shelled edamame
1 carton miso ginger broth (about 32 oz)
Optional for serving: green onion, sesame seeds, soy sauce

Instructions:
1. Sauté the mushrooms in a medium saucepan until golden and softened, then add the bok choy and cook until just wilted.
2. Pour in the broth, bring to a boil, then add in the edamame and wontons. Continue cooking for 4–6 minutes, until the wontons are heated through and tender.
