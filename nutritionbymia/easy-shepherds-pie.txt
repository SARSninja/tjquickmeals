recipe: Easy Shepherd's pie
author: instagram.com/nutritionbymia

Ingredients: 
1 lb. ground grass-fed beef
1 bag of frozen mashed potatoes 
2 cups organic foursome vegetable medley
1/2-1 cup shredded cheddar cheese 
1 cup turkey gravy (or enough to coat the beef mixture)

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 400 F.
2. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef until browned. Drain any excess fat.
3. Stir in the frozen vegetable medley and turkey gravy. Cook for 3-4 minutes until heated through and well combined.
4. Meanwhile cook the mashed potatoes. Place the contents of half the bag in a small saucepan and heat on low, stirring constraint for 3-4 minutes or until heated through.
5. Transfer the beef mixture to a small baking dish. Spread the mashed potatoes evenly over the top.
6. Sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese over the potatoes.
7. Bake for 15-20 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the filling is hot. Optional: Broil for 2-3 minutes for a golden top.
