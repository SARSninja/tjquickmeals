Cheesy chicken and broccoli rice bake

Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked basmati rice, rinsed
2 cups cooked chicken, shredded 
2 cups chicken broth
3 cups broccoli florets (fresh or frozen)
1 garlic & herbs Boursin cheese

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F.
2. In a baking dish, combine rice, chicken, broccoli, Boursin cheese and chicken broth. 
3. Bake for 30-40 minutes, until the rice is tender and most of the liquid is absorbed.
4. Fluff with a fork and serve warm.
