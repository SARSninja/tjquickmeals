recipe: Cheesy BBQ Chicken Avocado Roll-Ups
author: instagram.com/nutritionbymia

Ingredients:
4 string cheeses
4 medium whole grain tortillas
1 cup pre-cooked chicken, shredded
1/4 cup BBQ sauce
1 avocado, sliced

Instructions:
1. Toss shredded chicken with BBQ sauce until coated.
2. Lay out tortillas and add BBQ chicken down the center.
3. Place slices of avocado and string cheese on top of the chicken.
4. Roll tortillas up tightly, seam-side down.
5. Lightly spray with avocado oil and place in the air fryer basket.
6. Air fry at 400 F for 6-8 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden and crispy.
7. Slice and enjoy warm!
