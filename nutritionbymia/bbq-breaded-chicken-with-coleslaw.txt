recipe: BBQ breaded chicken with coleslaw
author: instagram.com/nutritionbymia

Ingredients:
2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/2 cup BBQ sauce
1 egg, whisked 
1 cup breadcrumbs
1 coleslaw kit

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F.
2. Pound chicken breasts to even thickness. Dip in whisked egg, then coat with breadcrumbs.
3. Place on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until cooked through and golden.
4. Brush BBQ sauce on top during the last 5 minutes of baking.
5. Toss coleslaw kit with included dressing and serve on the side.
