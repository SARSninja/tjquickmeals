recipe: Mediterranean Chicken Naan Wraps

Ingredients
4 pieces of naan
2 small cucumbers, diced
1 cup cherry or grape tomatoes, halved
1 package of shawarma chicken thighs
1 cup tzatziki
Optional for serving: extra tzatziki and parsley 

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 400Â°F and bake the shawarma chicken on a lined sheet pan for 15-18 minutes or until heated through and golden, then slice into strips
2. Warm the naan in a skillet or toaster oven until soft and lightly toasted, about 2-3 minutes
3. Halve the tomatoes and dice the cucumbers
4. Spread about Â¼ cup tzatziki on each naan, top with the tomatoes, cucumber and chicken.
5. Fold and serve as wraps.
