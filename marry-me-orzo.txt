recipe: Marry Me Orzo
author: instagram.com/nutritionbymia

Ingredients: 
1 bag of orzo
1 jar of sun-dried tomatoes
1 package of pre-cooked chicken, chopped up
2-3 cups of chopped spinach 
1 cup cream
(Optional for serving) fresh parmesan 

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 375 F. 
2. Place the orzo, sun-dried tomatoes with its oil, chicken, cream and 2 1/2 cups of water or chicken broth in a baking dish and cover.
3. Bake for 30 minutes.
4. Stir in the spinach before serving.
