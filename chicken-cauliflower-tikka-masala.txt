recipe: Chicken Cauliflower Tikka Masala
author: instagram.com/nutritionbymia

Ingredients:
1 bag cauliflower florets
1lb. chicken breast, cut into cubes
1/2 plain greek yogurt
1 package tikka masala curry sauce
2-3 pouches Pre-cooked Organic jasmine rice

Instructions:
1. In a medium bowl, season the cubed chicken with salt and pepper and toss with the Greek yogurt until evenly coated. Let sit for at least 10 minutes in the fridge while you prep the other ingredients.
2. Heat a drizzle of oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chicken and cook for 6-8 minutes, stirring occasionally, until browned and mostly cooked through.
Add the cauliflower florets directly to the skillet with the chicken. Cook for another 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the cauliflower begins to soften.
3. Pour the Tikka Masala Curry Sauce into the skillet with 1/2 cup of water. Stir to coat the chicken and cauliflower, lower the heat, and simmer for 5 minutes until everything is heated through and tender.
4. Microwave the  rice pouch according to package directions. Serve the chicken and cauliflower tikka masala over rice.
